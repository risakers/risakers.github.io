Evaluating radar reflectivity measurements as predictors of rainfall

NC State University
Computation for Undergraduates in Statistics Program, 2012-2013

This project gave me a chance to interact with a government agency and their data, enlightening myself to the power of the tools I was learning, as I was learning them. Meera Venkataraman and I worked on data processing for weeks and explored the efficiency of predictive models for months after. We took turns traveling locally and internationally to discuss our findings with others in our field. I presented at conferences in Raleigh, North Carolina; Seattle, Washington; and Montrèal, Quèbec, Canada.
